For Instructors

Use of facilities

FOR INSTRUCTORS TO ORGANIZE THEIR ACTIVITIES

Each year more and more instructors come to our centre to run their courses and freediving activities.

From Apnea Canarias we understand that it is difficult in some countries to find areas with good conditions for deep trainings. For these reasons we offer our facilities with all the necessary equipment to run courses and give to student all necessary material.

Prices depend on the number of students you bring with you. We have two options:

⋜ 4 STUDENTS: 15 € / day / student

+ 4 STUDENTS: 10 € / day / student

This amount only applies to students (not to instructors).

    Price includes:
  • – Classroom with capacity for 10 students, with screen and internet connection.
  • – General equipment: buoy and bottom weight.
  • – Hot showers and place to store belongings.
  • – Fixed lines at different depths training: -35m and -60m.
